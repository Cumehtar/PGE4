A significant Colovian city in the [[New Ayleid Imperium]] which is the origin point of the modern militarized [[Ayleid Revivalism]] movement after a major coup in 4E 335. Home to an excommunicated sect of Auriel worship which rejects the "Oneness" of the [[Archdiocese]]'s dogma.
