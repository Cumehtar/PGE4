"Knighthood" is an important position in certain polities, namely the [[New Ayleid Imperium]] and the [[Iliac League]]. In both in confers rights of citizenship. For the New Ayleids, Knightly Orders are religious militias which serve as a stepping stone to greater power and influence within the Imperium. In the Iliac League, Knights are the *de facto* rulers of the city-states in a sort of pseudo-democracy.
