The poetic name for religious syncretism of the [[Second Potentate]] that joins a specific interpretation of [[Neo-Alessianism]], the doctrines of the [[Benevolence of Mara]] in [[Bravil]], and Dunmeri-Nibenese Ancestor worship of the [[Arkayn Temple]] in [[Cheydinhal]].
